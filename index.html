Our Story

Koey's story

Koey (Kwan Yuen) lived in Hong Kong for over thirty years, where she built her life and career, working in marketing for a Scuba equipment manufacturer before joining the Antiquities and Monuments Office as an Assistant Heritage Officer. In 2021, she made the bold decision to move to the UK on her own to pursue new opportunities. Creative at heart, she loves drawing, particularly Japanese manga and animation.

Ian's story

Ian grew up in Clanfield and later moved to Hastings for work, where he built his career as an electronics engineer. A devoted football fan, he proudly supports Portsmouth FC. Outside of work, he is an avid gamer, and enjoys woodworking, having crafted several pieces of furniture within their home.

Our Relationship

They met in 2021 at Drallim, an engineering company in Hastings, the UK. Their paths crossed often in the canteen during lunchtime, and a quiet friendship slowly grew. They began dating in December 2023, became engaged in October 2025, and are now preparing to begin their married life together.

Travel

There is a free private car park with additional free on-street parking available on the adjacent roads. Hydro Hotel does have an EV charger for guest use.

The distance between Eastbourne train station and Hydro Hotel is 2 miles.
There are good taxi links (5 mins) to Eastbourne station (or a short walk for around 20 mins if the weather is nice).

Outfit suggestion


Eastbourne in September is expected to have mild, early‑autumn weather with average temperatures around 16–18°C during the day and cooler evenings around 12–14°C.

If the weather is kind, our ceremony will take place in the garden as shown in the picture. If the weather is unfavourable, the ceremony will move indoors, with heating on throughout to keep everyone warm and comfortable. Our wedding breakfast and reception will be entirely indoors.

We want you to feel completely comfortable on our special day, so there is no required outfit colour theme. If you’d like a little guidance, we kindly suggest wearing something elegant and celebratory, whatever makes you feel confident and joyful.
